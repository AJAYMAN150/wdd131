About Nara Park

Nara Park, located in the city of Nara, Japan, is famous for its freely roaming deer, historic temples, and beautiful landscapes. It is home to Todai-ji Temple, which houses the Great Buddha statue, and is a popular destination for cherry blossom viewing in spring.
